# Sample 04 – Invoice Attachment Scam

## Email Information

| Field | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Sample ID | 04 |
| Category | Malware Delivery |
| Source | Simulated phishing email for cybersecurity awareness |
| Target | Accounts Department |

---

## Email Details

**From:** Finance Team <accounts@invoice-payments.com>

**Reply-To:** accounts@invoice-payments.com

**Subject:** Outstanding Invoice - Immediate Payment Required

**Date:** 08 July 2026

**Attachment:** Invoice_8472.zip

---

## Email Body

Dear Customer,

Please find the attached invoice for your recent purchase.

The payment is overdue and must be completed immediately to avoid additional charges.

Kindly download the attached invoice and complete the payment today.

Regards,

Finance Department

---

## Initial Observation

The email pressures the recipient to open a ZIP attachment containing an invoice. Unexpected compressed attachments should always be treated as suspicious until verified.
