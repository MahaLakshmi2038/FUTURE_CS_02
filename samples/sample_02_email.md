## Email Body

Dear Customer,

We noticed unusual login activity on your online banking account.

To protect your account, we have temporarily restricted access.

Please verify your identity immediately using the secure link below:

https://secure-bank-verification.example

Failure to verify within **12 hours** may result in permanent account suspension.

Thank you,

**Online Banking Security Department**

---

## Initial Observation

The email appears to come from a banking institution and uses urgent language to encourage immediate action. Before interacting with the message, the sender, domain, and embedded link should be carefully verified.
