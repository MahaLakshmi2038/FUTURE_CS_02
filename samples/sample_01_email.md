# Sample 01 – Microsoft Account Verification Scam

## Email Information

| Field | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Sample ID | 01 |
| Category | Credential Phishing |
| Source | Simulated phishing email for cybersecurity awareness |
| Target | Microsoft Account Users |

---

## Email Details

**From:** Microsoft Support <support@micr0soft-security.com>

**Reply-To:** verify@secure-login-alert.com

**Subject:** Urgent: Your Microsoft Account Will Be Disabled

**Date:** 05 July 2026

---

## Email Body

Dear User,

We detected unusual activity on your Microsoft account.

Your account will be permanently disabled within **24 hours** unless you verify your identity immediately.

Click the link below to verify your account:

**https://micr0soft-login-verification.example**

Failure to verify may result in permanent loss of access.

Thank you,

**Microsoft Security Team**

---

## Initial Observation

At first glance, the email appears to be from Microsoft and attempts to create a sense of urgency by warning that the user's account will be disabled.

The email includes a verification link that should be treated with caution until its authenticity is confirmed through further investigation.
